What is the difference between Margin, Border and Padding?

People including myself get confused, because padding and margin both involve space around the element. Padding is the space between the element and it's border. Margin is the space between the outside and the border, normally the side of the page for example. Thankfully some smart people came up with a way to illustrate this!

Introducing the Box Model!

As you can see the box model is a picture representing all the things we are discussing.

The content in the box model could be anything from a div to your paragraph.

What are the role of values, empathy, and self-awareness in learning and programming?

I think the roles of values, empathy, and self-awareness are to not only your fellow programmers peers coachs etc, but to yourself.

They are there to really define and shape not only you as a person, but you as a workmate, a colleague and maybe a friend.

Values not only give you a way to view things, and a way to act, they also give people a way to relate and connect with you.

Empathy not only helps you be more mindful of other people and their circomstances, it also helps you intteract with yourself in a better manner. We've all heard the saying treat yourself as you would like others to be treated, whilst there's a lot of truth to this, the reality is thin gs are not so black and white. You can learn more about other people through empathy, and then cater your intteractions around that inforamtion, the truth is the best for you isn't always the best for others.

Self-awareness plays into all of these things, I'd argue they're all ( values, empathy and self-awareness) connected. Without self-awareness can you really have values? Or atleast outline ones you'd like to have / live by? Can you empathise with others if you can't with yourself maybe, but I think there would be a limit, although this could also be bias or ego talking?

The true aspect of selfawareness though, is knowing what you are doing how you are treating people how you are acting. You can find out is you're being hypocritical etc.
